About

Unleash Your Story with Agurchu Production! We're more than just a media company, we're a movement to bring your story to life, ignite your brand, and engage your audience like never before

Agurchu Production was derived from the brand Agurchu for graphic designs and branding, which was founded in 2019 by Katlego Jim Mtimane. Katlego, who is a talented graphic designer, web developer, video editor, director,photographer and music producer, started Agurchu with the goal of offering high-quality graphic design and branding services to clients. Over time, Katlego's passion for creating impactful and meaningful content expanded, leading him to partner with Lebowa Johannes Mtimane to transform the brand into a full-service media production and development company.

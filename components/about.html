The Story Behind the Stories

Get to know the talented team behind our inspiring and impactful content.

Agurchu Production was derived from the brand Agurchu for graphic designs and branding, which was founded in 2019 by Katlego Jim Mtimane. Katlego, who is a talented graphic designer, web developer, video editor, director,photographer and music producer, started Agurchu with the goal of offering high-quality graphic design and branding services to clients. Over time, Katlego's passion for creating impactful and meaningful content expanded, leading him to partner with Lebowa Johannes Mtimane to transform the brand into a full-service media production and development company.

Today, Agurchu Production specializes in creating high-quality, impactful content for a wide range of clients. From music videos and commercials to branded content, sound system and graphic designs, we have the expertise and experience to bring any project to life. Our team of talented filmmakers, writers, designers, web developers, and producers works closely with each client to understand their unique needs and deliver customized solutions that achieve their objectives.

Our competitive advantage lies in our combination of creative talent, technical expertise, and diversity. We use cutting-edge equipment and technology to produce high-quality, visually stunning content that engages audiences and delivers results. With a clear vision, a passion for storytelling, and a commitment to excellence, Agurchu Production is poised for success in the dynamic and ever-evolving media production industry.
